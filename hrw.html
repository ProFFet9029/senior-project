About Human Rights Watch

Human Rights Watch is an international non-governmental organization that works to protect human rights worldwide. They focus on holding governments accountable for their human rights abuses and supporting victims of injustice.

Charity Mission

Human Rights Watch's mission is to defend and protect human rights globally, focusing on issues like freedom of expression, women's rights, and protection of civilians during conflicts. They use research, advocacy, and partnerships to expose violations and drive meaningful change.
