About This Charity

Compassion International is dedicated to releasing children from poverty through holistic child development. Their programs provide education, healthcare, food security, and spiritual support to children in need.

Charity Mission

To release children from poverty in Jesus' name through comprehensive care and development programs.

Charity Impact

Compassion International allocates a substantial percentage of its funds to support child development programs, transforming lives and communities.
