About This Charity

CARE USA fights global poverty and provides disaster relief through innovative programs that improve education, healthcare, and economic opportunities. Their work focuses on empowering women and girls to create lasting change in communities.

Charity Mission

To save lives, defeat poverty, and achieve social justice by empowering women and their communities worldwide.

Charity Impact

CARE USA dedicates a large portion of its resources to programs that directly address poverty and inequality worldwide.
