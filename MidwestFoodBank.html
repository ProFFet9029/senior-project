About This Charity

Midwest Food Bank is dedicated to alleviating hunger and poverty by gathering and distributing food donations to nonprofit organizations and disaster sites. Through a vast network of volunteers and partners, they maximize the impact of every donation.

Charity Mission

To share the love of Christ by alleviating hunger and malnutrition locally and globally.

Charity Impact

Midwest Food Bank efficiently utilizes donations, turning every $1 into $60 worth of food distributed to those in need.
