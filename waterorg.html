Water.org

Water.org is a global nonprofit organization dedicated to addressing the water and sanitation crisis by empowering individuals and communities with access to safe water and sanitation. Founded by Gary White and Matt Damon, Water.org takes an innovative approach by combining philanthropy with financial tools like microloans through its WaterCredit initiative.

Charity Mission

Water.org's mission is to empower people in need by providing access to safe water and sanitation. The organization focuses on creating sustainable solutions through financial tools like microloans, enabling families to afford and maintain water and sanitation systems. By improving access to these essential resources, Water.org aims to break the cycle of poverty, improve health, and provide opportunities for education and economic growth.
