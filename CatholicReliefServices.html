About This Charity

Catholic Relief Services (CRS) provides emergency assistance, disaster relief, and long-term development aid to the world's most vulnerable populations. Their efforts span more than 100 countries, addressing poverty and injustice.

Charity Mission

To assist impoverished and disadvantaged people overseas, working in the spirit of Catholic social teaching to promote human dignity and justice.

Charity Impact

CRS is known for its program efficiency, dedicating the majority of its resources to aid programs that directly benefit communities.
