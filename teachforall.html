About Teach For All

Teach For All is a global network of organizations working to expand educational opportunity for children worldwide. They focus on developing leadership in classrooms and communities to address the most pressing educational inequities.

Charity Mission

Teach For All's mission is to build a global network of leaders who are committed to addressing educational inequity and ensuring all children have access to an excellent education. By supporting and empowering local organizations, they aim to create lasting change in education systems across the world.
