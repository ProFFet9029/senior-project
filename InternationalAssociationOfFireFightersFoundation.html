About International Association of Fire Fighters Foundation

The International Association of Fire Fighters Foundation is an organization with the purpose of supporting fire fighters, medical workers, and their families

Charity Mission

The IAFF Foundation assists IAFF fire fighters, emergency medical workers, and their families when they need it most. Our work supports burn survivors, funds critical behavioral health and occupational cancer programs, and gets immediate relief into the hands of our members impacted by disasters. We also honor the sacrifices of our fallen, the brave men and women who die in the line of duty, by supporting the IAFF Fallen Fire Fighter Memorial and offering scholarships to their survivors.
