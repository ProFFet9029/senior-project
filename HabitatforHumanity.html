About This Charity

Habitat for Humanity International partners with people around the world to help them build or improve a place they can call home. The organization focuses on affordable housing solutions and empowering communities through homeownership.

Charity Mission

To bring people together to build homes, communities, and hope, ensuring everyone has a decent place to live.

Charity Impact

Habitat for Humanity dedicates the majority of its resources to affordable housing programs, enabling long-term stability for families in need.
