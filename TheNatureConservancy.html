About This Charity

The Nature Conservancy is a global environmental organization dedicated to conserving the lands and waters on which all life depends. Through science-based initiatives, they address critical challenges such as climate change, biodiversity loss, and sustainable land and water management.

Charity Mission

To conserve the lands and waters on which all life depends.

Charity Impact

The Nature Conservancy allocates approximately 71.2% of its income to science-driven programs, ensuring that a significant portion of donations directly supports conservation efforts.
