About This Charity

MAP International is a global health organization that partners with people living in conditions of poverty to save lives and develop healthier families and communities.

Charity Mission

To provide medicines and health supplies to those in need so they might experience life to the fullest.

MAP International is noted for its high program expenditure, ensuring that donations are effectively used to provide medical aid.
