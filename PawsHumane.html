About Paws Humane Society

The Paws Humane Society is an organization whose goal is to serve as an Animal Resource Center uniting people and pets, building partnerships and creating solutions to become a no-kill community.

Charity Mission

Paws Humane Society is a private non-profit, no-kill animal shelter whose mission is to enrich the lives of both animals and people as a solution-based community resource for animal welfare by providing free or low cost spay/neuter, vaccinations, microchipping, essential veterinary services, rescue and adoption services, volunteer opportunities, community outreach, transportation, humane education, and our free community pet food pantry.
