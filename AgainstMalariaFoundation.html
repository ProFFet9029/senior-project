About This Charity

The Against Malaria Foundation (AMF) provides long-lasting insecticide-treated mosquito nets to protect people from malaria, one of the deadliest diseases worldwide. Their work saves lives and improves health outcomes in vulnerable communities.

Charity Mission

To prevent malaria by distributing effective mosquito nets and monitoring their usage to ensure maximum impact.

Charity Impact

AMF is highly rated for its cost-effectiveness and transparency, making a significant difference in malaria-endemic regions.

of donations go directly to providing mosquito nets
