About 350.org

350.org is a global grassroots movement focused on addressing the climate crisis. Its name refers to the target of 350 parts per million (ppm) of CO2 in the atmosphere, which is considered the safe limit for humanity. The organization mobilizes people around the world to advocate for climate action and reduce carbon emissions.
