About This Charity

Save the Children is a global humanitarian organization dedicated to improving the lives of children worldwide. They provide essential services in health, education, and protection to ensure that every child has the opportunity to survive, learn, and thrive.

Charity Mission

To inspire breakthroughs in the way the world treats children and to achieve immediate and lasting change in their lives.

Charity Impact

Save the Children operates in over 120 countries, ensuring that 85% of every dollar goes directly to programs that benefit children.
