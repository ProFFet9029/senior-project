Scholarship America

Scholarship America is a nonprofit organization focused on making education more accessible for students in need. They provide scholarships and educational support services to help students pursue higher education, regardless of their financial background.

Charity Mission

Scholarship America's mission is to make higher education accessible and affordable for students across the U.S., particularly those who face financial barriers. Their goal is to provide opportunities for all students to achieve their educational aspirations by offering scholarships, resources, and support to help them succeed. The organization partners with schools, corporations, foundations, and other organizations to offer financial assistance and guidance, making education more attainable for individuals from diverse backgrounds. Through their efforts, they strive to reduce the financial barriers that hinder educational attainment and foster a more equitable future for all students.
