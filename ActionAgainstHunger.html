About Action Against Hunger

Action Against Hunger's mission is to end hunger worldwide. The organization works to prevent, detect, and treat life-threatening malnutrition, especially among children, while addressing the underlying causes of hunger.

Charity Mission

Action Against Hunger is a global humanitarian organization focused on ending hunger and addressing its root causes, such as malnutrition, poor sanitation, and water scarcity. They provide life-saving programs to treat malnutrition, improve access to clean water and sanitation, and support food security. By combining emergency response with long-term solutions, the charity works to create sustainable change in vulnerable communities across the world.
