About This Charity

The Boys & Girls Clubs of America provides a safe and supportive environment for young people to learn, grow, and thrive. Their programs focus on academic success, good character, and healthy lifestyles for kids and teens nationwide.

Charity Mission

To enable all young people, especially those who need us most, to reach their full potential as productive, caring, and responsible citizens.

Charity Impact

Boys & Girls Clubs of America is recognized for its effective use of resources to support youth development programs across the United States.
