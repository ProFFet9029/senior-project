About This Charity

Semper Fi & America's Fund provides immediate financial assistance and lifetime support to combat wounded, critically ill, and catastrophically injured members of all branches of the U.S. Armed Forces and their families.

Charity Mission

To serve those who preserve our freedom by providing comprehensive programs that deliver critically needed resources to service members and their families.

Charity Impact

The Fund allocates approximately 93% of its spending toward programs, ensuring that the majority of donations directly support service members and their families.
