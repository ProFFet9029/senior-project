About This Charity

World Vision is a global humanitarian organization focused on improving the lives of children, families, and communities through emergency relief, education, economic development, and advocacy programs in over 90 countries.

Charity Mission

To work with children, families, and communities worldwide to reach their full potential by tackling the causes of poverty and injustice.

Charity Impact

World Vision dedicates a significant percentage of its resources to programs that address urgent needs and long-term solutions for global poverty.

of donations directly fund global programs
