About This Charity

The International Rescue Committee (IRC) responds to the world’s worst humanitarian crises, helping people to survive, recover, and rebuild their lives. Their programs support individuals and families affected by conflict and natural disasters worldwide.

Charity Mission

To help people whose lives and livelihoods are shattered by conflict and disaster to survive, recover, and gain control of their future.

Charity Impact

The IRC allocates a significant portion of its funds to on-the-ground programs, ensuring maximum impact in affected communities.

of donations support on-the-ground programs
