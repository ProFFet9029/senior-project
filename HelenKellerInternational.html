About This Charity

Helen Keller International is dedicated to preventing blindness and reducing malnutrition. Their work improves the lives of vulnerable populations around the world through evidence-based programs and partnerships.

Charity Mission

To save and improve the sight and lives of the most vulnerable and disadvantaged through programs designed to combat the causes and consequences of blindness, poor health, and malnutrition.

Charity Impact

Helen Keller International is recognized for its effective use of funds to deliver impactful health and nutrition programs worldwide.
