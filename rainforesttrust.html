About Rainforest Trust

Rainforest Trust works to protect rainforests and tropical habitats by partnering with local communities and conservation organizations. They focus on preserving biodiversity by creating protected areas for endangered species and combating deforestation.

Charity Mission

Rainforest Trust's mission is to protect threatened tropical forests and biodiversity through the establishment of protected areas. They achieve this by providing funding and expertise to local organizations, facilitating long-term conservation efforts and ensuring the survival of diverse ecosystems and species.

Charity Impact

95%
of donations go directly to creating and maintaining protected areas, fighting deforestation, and supporting local conservation projects in critical rainforest habitats worldwide.
