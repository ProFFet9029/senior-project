About This Charity

Good360 connects companies with excess goods to nonprofits in need, ensuring that surplus products are used for social good. Their mission helps reduce waste and supports communities worldwide.

Charity Mission

To help companies donate excess goods to charitable organizations, creating a meaningful impact while promoting sustainability.
