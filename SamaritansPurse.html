About This Charity

Samaritan's Purse is a Christian organization providing spiritual and physical aid to victims of war, poverty, natural disasters, disease, and famine. They aim to meet critical needs and share the love of Christ worldwide.

Charity Mission

To provide aid to people in need as an expression of Christian faith and to support individuals in rebuilding their lives.

Charity Impact

Samaritan's Purse is commended for its high program efficiency, ensuring a majority of its funds directly benefit those in need.
