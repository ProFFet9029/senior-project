About the NPCF

The National Pediatric Cancer Foundation aims to find low-risk, novel solutions to treating and possibly eliminate childhood cancer.

Charity Mission

By finding new, innovative, and low-toxin solutions to childhood cancer, parents would be able to find more methods of treating cancer that their children are afflicted with.

88.8%
of donations go to funding more research into new cancer treatments.
