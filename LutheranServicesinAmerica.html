About This Charity

Lutheran Services in America is a nationwide network of health and human services organizations that empower people in need. Their programs support seniors, children, families, and individuals with disabilities, promoting independence and well-being.

Charity Mission

To empower people in communities across America to live full, independent lives through a network of faith-based health and human services.

Charity Impact

Lutheran Services in America dedicates a significant portion of its resources to programs that provide critical services and support to vulnerable populations.
