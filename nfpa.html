About the NFPA

The National Fire Prevention Association helps make people aware of the risks of fire and how to prevent it.

Charity Mission

By helping make others more aware of fire and fire sources, such as rogue electricity and gas fumes, citizens can better take steps to prevent the risk of fire breaking out.

Charity Impact

65-70%
of donations go to making citizens more aware of fire risks and fire hazards in their everyday lives and how to prevent a fire breaking out.
