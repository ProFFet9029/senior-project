About This Charity

The Conservation Fund works to protect America's most critical lands and waters to provide greater access to nature, strengthen local economies, and enhance climate resiliency.

Charity Mission

To protect land and water resources for present and future generations by blending environmental stewardship with economic development.

Charity Impact

The Conservation Fund directs 96% of its funds toward program expenses, ensuring that the majority of donations support conservation efforts.
